BuzzerOperationMode

Specifies the repeats mode of sounding the buzzer.

  • BOM_DISABLE: Selects to turn off beep sound
  • BOM_EANBLE_NOREPEAT: Selects to sound the beep once
  • BOM_EANBLE_REPEAT: Selects to repeatly sound the beep
